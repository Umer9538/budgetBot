1. Introduction & Design Philosophy

Why These Colors?

BudgetBot's color system is designed to balance three critical needs: trust (essential for financial apps), warmth (to support emotional intelligence features), and innovation (to stand out in a crowded market).

Design Principles

  • Trust Through Color: Deep teal conveys financial stability without the clich√© of traditional blue
  • Emotional Connection: Warm coral and gold accents make finance approachable and human
  • Action-Oriented: High-energy accent colors inspire users to take control of their finances
  • Premium Feel: Sophisticated dark tones communicate quality and professionalism
  • Inclusive Design: Gender-neutral palette appeals to all target personas

Competitive Differentiation

Most finance apps fall into predictable color patterns:

Usage Guidelines

Primary (Deep Teal) - 60% Usage

  • Main navigation bars and headers
  • Primary buttons and CTAs
  • Key data visualizations
  • Budget progress indicators
  • App icon background

Secondary (Coral Orange) - 30% Usage

  • Important action buttons (Transfer, Pay, Save)
  • Alerts and notifications
  • Emotional spending indicators
  • Highlights in insights feed
  • Onboarding emphasis points

Accent (Warm Gold) - 10% Usage

  • Success celebrations and achievements
  • Gamification elements (badges, stars, streaks)
  • Premium feature highlights
  • Milestone completions
  • Savings goal progress
